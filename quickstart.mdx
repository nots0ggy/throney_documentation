## **Step 2: Set Up Roles for Throney**

After adding Throney, create a **dedicated role** that will serve as the **"Officer"** role. This role grants access to the **Throney Dashboard** for managing your guild and alliance.

## **Step 3: Initialize Throney in Your Server**

Once roles are created, use the following command in Discord to start the setup process:

```bash
/setup [alliance officer]
```

* Select the newly created **Alliance Officer** role.

  ![](/images/setupcommand.png "Setup Command")

* This will activate your **2-week trial** of Throney.

* You will see a **setup confirmation panel** like this:

![](/images/setupresponse.png)

<Info>
  **Note:** After the trial period, a subscription will be required to continue using Throney.
</Info>

***

## **Step 4: Access the Dashboard**

Once setup is complete, head over to the [Throney Dashboard](https://www.throney.gg) and log in.

* Click on your **Alliance**.

* You will see an interface similar to this:

![](/images/alliancedashboard.png)

* Click **"Update Guild"** and select the **correct member role** for each guild.

* By default, only **one guild** is created. You can **rename it** or **add additional guilds** as needed.

<Warning>
  Any updates to roles or changes in who has the officer roles will take up to 5 minutes to reflect. Alternatively, you can re-run the `/setup` command with the same role to apply the changes immediately. This is due to an efficiency limitation‚Äîthank you for your patience!
</Warning>

## **Step 5: Upload Your Character Data**

To start tracking members and event participation, **upload your character profile** by running the following command in Discord:

```bash
/upload
```

* Attach a **clear screenshot** of your character page, ensuring all relevant details are visible.

* A valid character profile should look like this:

![](/images/characterprofile.jpg)

<Tip>
  Ensure that **your class, level, and name** are clearly visible on the left-hand side of the screen.
</Tip>
