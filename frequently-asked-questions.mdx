<Accordion title="How does Throneyâ€™s DKP system work?" icon="chart-line" iconType="solid">
Officers can assign **DKP values** to events. Members **earn DKP** when **attendance is logged** via `/attendance`.

<Accordion title="How do I adjust a player's DKP?" icon="scale-balanced" iconType="solid">
Use `/adjust award @user [amount]` to **add DKP** or `/adjust deduct @user [amount]` to **remove DKP**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I post an item for Upgrade/Trait/Litho bidding?" icon="hammer" iconType="solid">
1. Go to the **Alliance Dashboard** and navigate to **Item Listings**.
2. Click **"Add New Item"**.
3. Select the **text channel**, **item name**, and any notes.
4. Click **Submit** to post the item to Discord.

You can also upload guild storage screenshots through the **Loot Bank** to extract and route items in bulk.
</Accordion>

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting & Support

<Accordion title="My gear isn't uploading correctly. What should I do?" icon="triangle-exclamation" iconType="solid">
- Ensure the **entire character screen is visible** in your screenshot, including class name, level, and stats.
- Try re-uploading using `/upload` with a slightly different screenshot (different aspect ratio or zoom level).
- If it still fails, an officer can use `/edit` to correct any mistakes.
- Throney supports **English and Portuguese** class names natively. Other languages are detected through weapon combination matching.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can officers upload gear for members?" icon="user-shield" iconType="solid">
Yes, use `/upload [image] @user` to upload a character profile on behalf of another member.
</Accordion>

Contact **support in our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/ecrYsMDvSf)** for assistance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I find tutorials on how to use Throney?" icon="circle-play" iconType="solid">

<Accordion title="What languages does Throney support?" icon="language" iconType="solid">
The `/upload` command natively supports **English and Portuguese** class names. Players using other game languages (German, French, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, etc.) will still have their class detected correctly through weapon combination matching. Player names with special characters may need manual correction via `/edit`.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the Loot Bank?" icon="box-archive" iconType="solid">
The Loot Bank lets officers upload screenshots of their guild's item storage chest. Throney's AI extracts all visible items and matches them against the game database. You can then route extracted items directly to auctions or item listings in bulk.
</Accordion>
